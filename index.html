Our Mission

Charity: Water was founded on a simple but powerful belief: access to clean water changes everything. What began as a mission to bring safe drinking water to communities in need has grown into a global movement fueled by transparency, innovation, and compassion.

Real Impact: Meet Namazzi

Namazzi from Uganda used to have limited water to use for bathe her child in the mornings. However, with donations from Charity: Water Namazzi can now rest assured with clean water to properly bathe her child everyday.
